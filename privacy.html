Privacy

Lumina is a private morning voice ritual delivered through Telegram. This page explains the simple privacy posture for the current static marketing site and Telegram-first flow.

What the website collects

The website may use privacy-conscious analytics and simple A/B tests to understand what helps visitors start Lumina in Telegram. This can include page visits, referral source, CTA variant, and CTA clicks. It does not ask for your name, email, card, or health information on this website.

Standard server/CDN logs may also be created by GitHub Pages, Cloudflare, Telegram, or your browser/network provider when you visit or click a link.

What Telegram may share with Lumina

When you start Lumina in Telegram, Telegram may provide basic chat information needed for the bot to reply, such as your Telegram user/chat identifier, username if available, messages you send to the bot, and timestamps.

Payment information

Payments are handled by Stripe. Lumina does not store full card numbers. Stripe may process payment details according to Stripe’s own privacy and security practices.

How information is used

  • To send your free sample and daily Lumina voice notes.
  • To provide support, billing help, privacy help, or refunds.
  • To keep the service safe and reliable.

Important note

Lumina is not therapy, medical care, or crisis support. Please contact local emergency services or a qualified professional if you need urgent help.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact Lumina through Telegram for now. A dedicated support email can be added here when finalized.
